Madone SL

Madone SL is a race-ready aero road bike that beats the wind and keeps you smooth on rough roads and your longest, fastest rides. It has the same exact integration, tube shapes, and road-smoothing IsoSpeed as the top-tier Madone SLR, with a 500 Series OCLV Carbon frame that trades a few grams in weight for some extra cash in your pocket.
